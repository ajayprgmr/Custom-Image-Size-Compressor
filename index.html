This is an image compressor website, here you can compress your images accoding to your desired size range. it can compress the image size upto 90% the orignal size.

How to use it?

  • Upload the image using upload button or drag and drop area.
  • Scroll the image-size-bar to the left or right and choose your desired size range, while scrolling the size of the compressed image would be shown on just right of upload section with file size.
  • Now, when you found your size range click on the Download button to download the compressed image.
